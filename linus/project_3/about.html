2015 Voices

In this lecture series, we have invited three established innovators in the food industry. The three speakers: Jamie Oliver , a British celebrity chef who is now also heading a global campaign for better food education; David Chang, founder of the highly popular and innovative restaurant chain Momofuku that is now taking over the culinary world by storm with his creative yet daring dishes; and finally Ethan Brown, founder of Beyond Meat, whose mission is to create meat alternative with plant protein in an attempt to improve human health and the negative impacts of the meat industry on the environment. Coming from three different but tightly related backgrounds, the three speakers will offer us their unique perspectives on the very basic but complex subject of food.
