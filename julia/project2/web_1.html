    MINISTRY OF ECONOMICAL AFFAIRS

    LUST designed the more than 30 meter wide light installation for the main entree of the Ministry of Economical Affairs in The Hague. The installation makes use of the semi-transparant albast wall above the main lobby to create a efemeral effect; when the installation is off, there are no LED-lights visible at all.

    The lights are more condense in the middle of the hall, and have more and more space between them going to the outside, creating a spatial effect.

    The installation displays moving patterns and short messages in a number of custom-designed typefaces, which can be controlled through a custom-build CMS (Content Management System).
