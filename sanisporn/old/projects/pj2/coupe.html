Coupé/Décalé


Year

2011

Materials

35mm film transferred on Betanum

Dimension

5' 20"

Description

Coupé/Décalé is an experimental film that alludes to an ethnological style, but which has been cut manually – literally and figuratively – in order to create a slight distance. Through this manipulation, a continuous line appears, separating the image into two parts, one second out of sync.
