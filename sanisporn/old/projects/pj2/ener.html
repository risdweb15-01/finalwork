Energeia Akinesis


Year

2010

Materials

Rubber, steel, hoist

Dimension

240 x 230 cm

Description

“Energeia akinesis” means energy without movement. This sculpture is inspired by the chandelier "Crown of Light" designed by Viollet-Le-Duc installed in Notre-Dame de Paris at the time of its restoration in the 19th century; the chandelier received harsh criticism at the time and Viollet-Le-Duc was accused of adulterating the legacy of the church.
