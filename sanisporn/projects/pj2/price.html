Selected Works

The Price of Danger


Year

2010

Materials

Black steel and cross belt

Dimension

Variable dimensions

Description

Airplane wings are a symbol of a modern movement, the explosion of fluxuations and of reduced distances between remote societies. They invite questions about the role that technology plays in our concept of modernity and suggest that our devotion to science can be seen as the remains of an archaic system of thought.
