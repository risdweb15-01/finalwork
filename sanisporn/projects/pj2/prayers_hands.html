Camille Henrot's
Selected Works

Prayer's Hands


Year

2010

Materials

Black steel and cross belt

Dimension

202 x 90 x 50 cm

Description

A safety harness is encased in a structure reminiscent of stained glass. Like Tevau, the harness is an object that provides protection against a basic danger. As a figure of overlapping and interleaving, this object could have a religious function, given that the latin etymology for religion is "to bind" or "to connect".
